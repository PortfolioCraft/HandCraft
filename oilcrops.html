Oil Palm

This is our long term project where by we help farmers acquire seedlings affordably. This value chain will ensure farmers have sustainable income as it matures within 4 years and is harvested for 35 years before it's lifespan lapses. This is a very versatile crop as our of its oil farmer can make their own soap use its leaves to weave, animal fodder and even source of energy. This is targeting Western Kenya region and the coast . This crop will also help increase forest coverage in the regions.

Sunflower 

This is a short term crop that we aim to have farmers grow in blocks. This crop matures faster and has very high oil content. With production the income of farmers will be diversified and at the same time leverage on the protein supplement for animal feeds from the cakes after pressing. We are introducing this as an alternative short term oil crop to help keep the farmer going as they are waiting for palm to mature.

Canola Oil

This is another value chain we are doing with our farmers in western kenya. We have partners and collaborators we are working with for the offtake. These value chains will help keep farmers busy and also create jobs for women and youths . By these initiative there will be improved livelihood due to increased disposable income.
