HANDKRAFT FOODS LIMITED is a limited company that strives to grow, process and trade in Crops and Livestock both in the local and export markets.

About Us

Who We Are

The mission and vision of Handkraft Foods Limited is to work with farmers to empower them to embrace commercial farming of crops and Livestock.

 

Handkraft Foods Limited works with international traders who provide the market for the produce from farmers and our partners provide technical support to ensure quality of produce.

 

Traceability is key to food safety and Handkraft Foods Limited works with farmers and partners to ensure it is guaranteed.
